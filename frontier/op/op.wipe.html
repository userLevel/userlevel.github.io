op.wipe

Syntax op.wipe ()
Params None required
Action Deletes every heading in the target outline, leaving a single, blank summit.
Returns True
Examples With either examples.Sample Outline selected, execute:
op.wipe ()


   » true

and observe the result.

Notes op.wipe performs a Clear operation on the outline, not a Cut. The contents of the outline are therefore not available to paste into another outline.
See Also op.deleteLine
